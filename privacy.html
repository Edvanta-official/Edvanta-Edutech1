Data Privacy & Security

Privacy Policy

Learn how Edvanta collects, uses, and safeguards your personal information when using our Website and Learning Management Portal.

🔒 Introduction & Overview

This Privacy Policy forms part of Edvanta’s Terms and Conditions for accessing our Website and Learning Management Portal. By using our Website or LMS, you expressly agree to our collection and use of your personal data as outlined in this Privacy Policy. If we request information that can identify you, you can be assured it will be used strictly in accordance with this statement. You should review this page periodically to ensure you are aware of any updates.

By accessing our site and services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy & Data Policy and expressly consent to the processing of your personal information as described herein.

📋 Information We Collect

To provide our online courses, weekend live sessions, certificates, internship programs, and project opportunities, we collect the minimum information required to create and manage your account. This includes:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Password
  • Age (to verify you are 16 or older)
  • Phone number (only when necessary for communication)
  • Academic or professional details (if required for internships/projects)

Note: We do not store any payment-related information. All payments are securely processed via Razorpay.

🍪 Use of Cookies, Tags & Device Identifiers

Cookies are small data files stored in your browser. Device identifiers uniquely identify devices such as mobile phones.

We may use:

  • Cookies
  • Web beacons/pixels
  • Device identifiers

Certain features may only work when cookies are enabled. You may disable cookies through your browser settings, but some Edvanta features may become inaccessible.

💬 Feedback

If you provide feedback, we may use it for improving our services. Personal data within the feedback remains protected under this Privacy Policy.

⚙️ Use of Personal Information

We primarily use your information to:

  • Provide course access and LMS services
  • Issue certificates for course completion, internships, and projects
  • Respond to support queries
  • Improve platform functionality
  • Detect and resolve issues
  • Ensure compliance with terms of use
  • Communicate updates, opportunities, and service announcements

Customer support representatives may access your information solely to assist you. We may also verify information with third parties for accuracy or legal compliance.

📊 Monitoring User Activity

We may monitor activity on our systems for:

  • Improving performance
  • Ensuring compliance with our Terms of Use
  • Supporting users
  • Developing new features and functionalities

📧 Managing Your Information

We may contact you with:

  • Administrative emails
  • Course-related communications
  • Customer service messages
  • Promotional communications (optional)

You may opt out of promotional messages at any time. Administrative communications cannot be opted out of unless the account is deleted.

🛡️ Children’s Privacy

Edvanta does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. We do not target or communicate with users below this age. If information belonging to a child under 16 is discovered, it will be deleted immediately.

🔐 Security & Retention

We take reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. Data is stored securely on compliant servers within India or approved cloud environments.

We retain data as long as your account remains active or as required for certification and service-related purposes.

🔗 Third-Party Sites

Our Website or LMS may contain links to third-party platforms for convenience. Edvanta does not control or endorse these websites and is not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage reviewing their privacy statements separately.

📝 Changes to This Policy

As technology and our services evolve, we may update this Privacy & Data Policy. We will post updated versions on this page and retain prior versions for reference. Material changes will be communicated via email.

📈 Reports, Analysis & Confidentiality

Edvanta may use anonymized, aggregated, or non-identifiable data for research, statistical analysis, business intelligence, and improving services. All Edvanta employees and data processors handling sensitive information are obligated to maintain strict confidentiality.

📍 Location Information

We may collect approximate location information (such as country) using your IP address for regional personalization. We do not collect precise GPS-based location. You may disable location services on your device to restrict this.

✏️ Your Choices & Data Accuracy

You may update your account information anytime. If your information is inaccurate or outdated, please correct it or contact us for assistance. To protect your data, we may verify identity before making changes.
